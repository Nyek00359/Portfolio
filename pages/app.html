PrescripTalk

A prescription is an official written order from a licensed healthcare provider for a patient to receive specific medication or treatment. Traditionally, prescriptions have been handwritten, but this method has drawbacks that can compromise safety. so we decided to develop an app that can convert handwritten to digital prescriptions.
